Project

Truss Bridge

Time
4 weeks
Constraints
Materials/Tools: Glue, cement glue, file folders, Exacto knives.
Learning Objectives
  • Identify variables contributing to bridge strength.
  • Reason about geometry and weight distribution.
  • Read and interpret models and blueprints.
  • Reason about multivariable systems.
  • Use mathematical reasoning to strategize and execute.
  • Use engineering language to collaborate.
social Roles
Civil engineer
Collaborative Roles
Planner, Problem-Solver, Project Manager, Evaluator
